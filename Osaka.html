Osaka
21/09/2026 calendar_month

Osaka is one of Japan’s most vibrant and dynamic cities, known for its friendly atmosphere, modern architecture and rich cultural heritage.

Famous for its street food, lively neighborhoods and historical landmarks, Osaka offers a perfect mix of tradition, entertainment and urban life.

Osaka Bridges
22/09/2026 calendar_month

Osaka is crossed by numerous rivers and canals, connected by iconic bridges, especially in areas like Dotonbori and Nakanoshima.

Walking across these bridges offers stunning city views, neon lights at night and a glimpse into Osaka’s energetic urban lifestyle.

Shitennoji Temple
27/09/2026 calendar_month

Shitennoji Temple is one of Japan’s oldest Buddhist temples and a key spiritual landmark in Osaka.

Founded in the 6th century, it represents centuries of history, tradition and religious devotion, making it a must-visit cultural site.

Osaka Castle
30/09/2026 calendar_month

Osaka Castle is a historic landmark and one of Japan’s most famous castles, symbolizing the power and heritage of Osaka.

Originally built in the 16th century, it has been reconstructed multiple times, surrounded by beautiful parks and cherry blossoms, making it a must-visit site for history and nature lovers.
